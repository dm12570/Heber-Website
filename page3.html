Heber valley rail road is one of the better things to see when you come to heber.
The heber Valley rail road has a great assortment of activities which they do year round.
One of these being the Heber Creeper during halloween, and during the christmas time, the
staff dress up as elves and Santa and ms.Claus.


The ice Castles are another thing you could see during any winter time in the future or here today.
During the late fall and early winter, staff members create the ice castles by using a variety of
different tools with the most important being water (shocking!!!).


Dairy Keen is one of the best places, in my opinion, to be all year round.
The food is extremely good and the shakes are great. If you have your family
with you, you can sit outside in the toy train fit with seats and tables inside.
